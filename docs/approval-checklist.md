# Approval Checklist

Updated: 2026-04-24

Use this before any content goes live, any profile changes are saved, or any listing is published.

---

## Before Posting Any Content

- [ ] Is the claim in the content accurate?
- [ ] Is there any income guarantee or implied guaranteed return? (If yes — remove it)
- [ ] Is the AI visual disclosed as AI brand creative, not a real person?
- [ ] Is the CTA link pointing to the correct Gumroad or destination?
- [ ] Is the caption under 150 characters for TikTok or link-first for X?
- [ ] Did Ed explicitly say this batch is approved to post?

---

## Before Updating Any Profile

- [ ] Is the new profile image generated and saved locally first?
- [ ] Is the bio copy reviewed and finalized?
- [ ] Is the link correct?
- [ ] Did Ed explicitly confirm the update for this specific platform?

---

## Before Publishing Any Listing

- [ ] Is the offer copy complete (headline, bullets, price, CTA)?
- [ ] Is the product file attached correctly?
- [ ] Is the price confirmed?
- [ ] Did Ed explicitly confirm this listing is ready to publish?

---

## Before Sending Any Email

- [ ] Is the recipient confirmed by Ed?
- [ ] Is the content reviewed by Ed?
- [ ] Did Ed say "send it" explicitly in this session?

---

## Before Any OAuth / App Connection

- [ ] Did Ed explicitly initiate this connection?
- [ ] Has the scope of the connection been explained?
- [ ] Did Ed confirm "connect it" right before the action?

---

## Content Safety Review (For Every Slideshow or Post)

- [ ] No fake testimonials
- [ ] No fake earnings screenshots framed as personal proof
- [ ] No income guarantees
- [ ] No deceptive identity claims (AI persona clearly labeled or understood as brand creative)
- [ ] No political manipulation or religious coercion
- [ ] No exaggerated urgency ("last chance", "ends tonight") without a real deadline
