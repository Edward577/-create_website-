## Consistency Rules

1. Keep age, hairstyle family, and face shape stable across batches.
2. Keep wardrobe modest and repeat navy/cream as the core palette.
3. Keep lighting soft and daylight-based, not neon or heavy studio glam.
4. Keep expressions calm, serious, and approachable.
5. Prefer desk, laptop, notebook, or neutral room context over fantasy scenes.
6. Avoid overt partisan props. The tone should read conservative through restraint and values, not costume symbols.
7. Use transparent AI-brand-creative disclosure where needed.
