## STYLE CONSISTENCY RULES

To recreate the same woman across generations:

1. **Hair:** Light brown to dark blonde. Natural. Down or simply pulled back. No extreme styles.
2. **Eyes:** Clear, realistic, brown or hazel. Not cartoonishly bright or oversized.
3. **Skin:** Clean, natural, light. Minimal makeup. No heavy contouring.
4. **Age:** Late 20s to early 30s. Never younger. Never older than mid-30s.
5. **Clothing:** Navy blazer or structured navy/cream top is the default. Modest neckline always.
6. **Lighting:** Soft natural daylight, warm-neutral. Always from the left when possible.
7. **Background:** Muted neutral — cream, white, light gray, or soft office setting. Never dark or dramatic.
8. **Expression:** Calm, composed, intelligent. Mild natural smile or neutral. Never dramatic or exaggerated.
9. **Lens feel:** 85mm or 50mm portrait. No fish-eye, no ultra-wide distortion.
10. **Color palette:** Navy `#1B2B4B`, cream `#F5F0E8`, warm gold `#C9A84C` (accent only).

## WHERE TO GENERATE

Claude Code cannot generate images directly. Use one of these:

| Tool | Best For | Prompt Format |
|---|---|---|
| **ChatGPT / DALL-E 3** | Quick drafts, conceptual | Paste prompts above directly |
| **Midjourney v6** | Highest realism and consistency | Add `--ar 1:1` or `--ar 9:16` to end |
| **Flux (via Replicate or Fal.ai)** | Photorealistic, best for profile images | Paste prompts above |
| **Adobe Firefly** | Safe commercial license | Paste prompts above |

For Midjourney, append these parameters to any prompt:
```
--style raw --ar 1:1 --v 6.1 --cref [paste URL of approved image to lock face]
```

Once a first approved image exists, use `--cref` with that image URL to maintain consistency across all future generations.
